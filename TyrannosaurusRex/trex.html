What is the Tyrannosaurus Rex?

The name Tyrannosaurus rex originates from the latin word meaning 'Tyrant Lizard King'.
The trex roamed the planet about 65 million years ago during the cretaceous period

Where was it located?

It lived throughout what is know known as Western USA, but what was then known as an Island continent known as Laramdia
There is evidence to suggest they hunted and lived in packs

Biology of Tyrannosaurus Rexs

The trex is the heaviest and strongest land carnivore ever to evolve.
Trex's could've exceeded sizes of 13m tall
They were a bipedal carnivore with a giant skull with a long tail to balance

How did the Trex hunt?

It had 60 8-inch long teeth, and its jaw had enough force to crush a car
Although small, they used their claws to slash prey
They could reach speeds up to 20km/h, out running most humans
